Predator Accuracy.1 Parley Pack

The Predator Accuracy.1 is designed for precision and control. These were my first "top of the line" soccer cleats. To be honest, at first it didn't fit my foot shape but over time the synthetic upper molded and it became my best fitting cleat. It may just be how much I use them but they don't hold up very long as the rubber strike elements fall off and the soleplate becomes less aggressive. To learn more, click the image above.

Predator Elite FG White Gold

These are my best-looking pair of cleats. Haven't really had the heart to wear them much, but they are so nice. This is the "upgraded" version of the predator.1. Click the image above for more details.

Mercurial Vapor 16 Elite AG Blackout

These cleats are about speed. The main reason I bought them was because they have Grip Knit which is usually only found in more control boots like the Phantom GX2 (see below). Although the AG studs are not quite as aggressive as the FG variant, it helps a lot more with changes in direction and can still be used on FG surfaces. Click the image above for more details.

Phantom GX2 FG LV8

These are by far my most comfortable pair of cleats. They fit like a sock and feel as if I have so much grip on the ball. This features Nike's Grip Knit technology, the same as the Vapor 16 just up a few notches. They feel like I have latex on with how grippy they are. Great for rainy days when you need that little extra grip. Click the image above to learn more.
